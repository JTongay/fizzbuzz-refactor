Rules of Fizzbuzz!
  • If a number is divisible by 3, the word "fizz" will appear.
  • If a number is divisible by 5, the word "buzz" will appear.
  • If a number is divisible by both 3 and 5, the word "fizzbuzz" will appear.
  • To play, enter a number between 1 and 100 in the text box and click submit, or hit enter.
  • To start over, click clear or hit the escape key.
